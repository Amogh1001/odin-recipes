Sandwich

Description

This is an easy recipe. Can be made by a child

That is, if you are not stupid lol.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • An onion
  • A tomato
  • 20 grams of butter
  • A slice of cheese
  • Some sort of sauce, any would be fine, ig.

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and tomato.
  2. Add the slice of cheese to a slice of bread.
  3. Spread the sauce on the other slice.
  4. Put the diced onion and tomato on the slice with the sauce.
  5. Toast the stack with butter.
